Chart Selection Justification

1. Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- **Question:** How are sales evolving over time?
- **Why:** Line charts clearly show trends and seasonality
- **Design:** Continuous time axis
- **Avoided:** Bar chart for time trend

---

2. Regional Performance (Map/Bar Chart)
- **Question:** Which regions perform best?
- **Why:** Geographic visualization enhances spatial understanding
- **Design:** Color indicates profit margin
- **Avoided:** Pie chart (not scalable)

---

3. Category Profitability (Bar Chart)
- **Question:** Which categories drive profit or loss?
- **Why:** Bars effectively compare magnitude
- **Design:** Red/green color coding
- **Avoided:** Treemap (less precise comparisons)

---

4. Customer Segment Analysis (Bar Chart)
- **Question:** Which segment is most profitable?
- **Why:** Clear comparison across segments
- **Design:** Sorted by profit margin
- **Avoided:** Pie chart

---

5. Discount vs Profit (Scatter Plot)
- **Question:** How does discount impact profit?
- **Why:** Shows relationship and correlation
- **Design:** Size = sales, color = category
- **Avoided:** Line chart (misleading trend)

---

6. Shipping Performance (Bar Chart)
- **Question:** Which shipping modes cause delays?
- **Why:** Easy comparison of delivery buckets
- **Design:** Grouped by delay category
- **Avoided:** Scatter (less clarity)

---

7. Return Analysis (Bar/Heatmap)
- **Question:** Where are returns highest?
- **Why:** Clear comparison of return rates
- **Design:** Highlight high-risk areas
- **Avoided:** Pie chart
