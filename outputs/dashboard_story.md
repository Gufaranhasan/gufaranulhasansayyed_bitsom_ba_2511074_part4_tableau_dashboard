Executive Dashboard Story

1. Executive Summary
The business demonstrates strong revenue performance but faces challenges in profitability due to discounting and operational inefficiencies.

---

2. What Is Performing Well
- Technology category is a major revenue driver
- Certain regions contribute significantly to sales and profit
- Customer demand exists across segments

---

3. What Is Underperforming
- Furniture category margins are weak
- High discount transactions reduce profitability
- Delayed shipments affect customer experience

---

4. Key Risks
- Margin erosion due to excessive discounting
- Operational inefficiencies in delivery
- Hidden losses in high-volume categories

---

5. Opportunities
- Rationalize discount strategy
- Improve logistics efficiency
- Focus on high-profit segments

---

6. Recommended Actions
1. Implement discount governance policies
2. Improve delivery timelines and SLAs
3. Identify and eliminate loss-making SKUs
4. Monitor region-wise profitability closely

---

7. Limitations
- No detailed cost breakdown
- No customer lifetime value data
- No inventory insights

---

8. Next Steps
